Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients (for 8 pers.)

  • Butter: 125g
  • Flour: 100g
  • Black pepper
  • Salt
  • Grated cheese: 70g
  • Nutmeg (grated): 3 pinches
  • Thyme
  • Laurel: 2 leaves
  • Red wine: 200mL
  • Tomato puree: 800g
  • Carrot: 1
  • White onion: 3
  • Lasagna sheets: 1 pack
  • Garlic: 2 cloves
  • Celery: 1 stick
  • Beef mince: 600g
  • Water: 150mL
  • Basil
  • Parmiggiano cheese: 125g
  • Milk: 1L

Cooking steps

  1. Blanche the garlic and the chopped onions in a little olive oil.
  2. Add the carrot and the chopped celery stick, then add the meat and let cook.
  3. After a few minutes, add the red wine. Let cook until the liquid evaporates.
  4. Mix in the tomato puree, the water, and the herbs. Add salt and pepper to taste and leave to simmer on a small fire for 45min.
  5. Start preparing the bechamel sauce: Melt 100g of butter.
  6. Off the fire, add all the flour in one go.
  7. Put the pot back to the fire and mix until the liquid starts thickening.
  8. Mix in the nutmeg, add slat and pepper. Leave to cook for around 5min on a very small fire, keep mixing. Set aside
  9. Preheat the oven at 200C. Oil the lasagna dish. Set one lasagna sheet at the bottom, then one layer of bechamel, another sheet, then bolognese, bechamel, and parmiggiano. Add layers until you run out of ingredients.
  10. For the last layer, only put bechamel and cover with grated cheese. Add some chunks of butter.
  11. Cook for about 25min.
  12. Enjoy!

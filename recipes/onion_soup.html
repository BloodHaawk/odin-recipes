Onion Soup Recipe

Ingredients (for 4 pers.)

  • Black pepper
  • Salt
  • Comte cheese (grate): 100g
  • Flour: 1tbspn
  • Oil: 1tbspn
  • Butter: 50g
  • White onions: 4
  • White wine: 250mL
  • Water: 1L
  • Soft bread: 6 slices

Cooking steps

  1. Peel and chop the onions.
  2. Fry the onions in oil and butter until they become tender and golden.
  3. Add the flour, warm water and wine. Season to taste.
  4. Cover and leave to simmer for 20min.
  5. Toast the bread.
  6. Set the slices in 4 inidividual oven-safe bowls.
  7. Sprinkle some grated cheese, and add the soup on top.
  8. Add more cheese cheese on top, and put under the grill until golden.

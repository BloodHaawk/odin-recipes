Crepes Recipe

Description

This is going to be very good with some jams or even served as a savoury dish, with ham and cheese.

Ingredients (for 15 crepes)

  • Rum: 50mL
  • Melted butter: 50g
  • Oil: 2tbspn
  • Sugar: 3tbspn
  • Flour: 300g
  • Eggs: 3
  • Milk: 600mL

Cooking steps

  1. Put the flour in a mixing bowl, and scuplt a well in the center.
  2. Put the eggs, sugar, oil and teh melted butter in that well.
  3. Slowly mix everything with a whisk, while slowly adding milk. The batter should a the consistency of a slightly thick liquid.
  4. Add in the rum.
